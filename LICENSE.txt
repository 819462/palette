ACADEMIC CREDIT
---------------
The core technique implemented in this software — the repurposing of
speaker/headphone drivers as microphone input devices via audio codec
pin retasking — was originally demonstrated by researchers at Ben-Gurion
University of the Negev in their 2016 paper:

    "SPEAKE(a)R: Turn Speakers to Microphones for Fun and Profit"
    Mordechai Guri, Yosef Solewicz, Andrey Daidakulov, Yuval Elovici
    Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, Beer-Sheva, Israel

This software is an independent implementation inspired by that research.
